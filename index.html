Timothy T. Joe

Software Developer

Timothy T. Joe, Advance Certificate (Liberia), is a Liberian Software Developer, CSO Volunteer, and Community Services enthusiast. Currently serving as Communication Officer at Compassion for Humanity (C4H Liberia) on the Countrywide Sanitation Activities (CWSA) project funded by USAID/PSI to advocate for the establishment of a standing committee on WASH at the national Legislatural. He speaks and writes fluent English.

He started his software development journey building website with WordPress, Vanilla CSS and JavaScript, Bootstrap and JQuery. His first major challenge was to build a Content Management System for Rotary Club of Sinkor which he did with Vanilla PHP back in 2019. Since then has delved into many technologies and Languages such as JAVA, C#, Python etc. and he had practiced building Single Page Apppication. His current favorite stack is the MERN which consit of MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJs and NodeJS...

During his free time he work on side project and looking for ways to expand his understand of technologies and the world around him.
